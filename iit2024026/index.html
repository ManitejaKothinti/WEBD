About us

We are Maniteja and Shankar, professional editors with a passion for crafting visually stunning content.
With years of experience in the editing industry, we have honed our skills in a variety of
software and techniques, ensuring each project is executed to perfection. Our educational backgrounds
have provided us with a strong foundation in both technical and creative aspects, making us versatile in
the field. As the founders of Classic Studio, we are dedicated to delivering top-quality work and bringing
our clients' visions to life. Our commitment to excellence drives everything we do.

Services

At Classic Studio, we offer a wide range of professional editing services tailored to meet the unique needs of our clients.
From video editing and color grading to audio mixing and motion graphics, we bring creativity and precision to every project.
Our expertise also extends to post-production for films, commercials, and social media content, ensuring a polished and
impactful final product. We work closely with clients to understand their vision and deliver exceptional results. Whether
you're an individual creator or a large business, we are committed to making your content stand out.
